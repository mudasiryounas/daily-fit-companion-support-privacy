1. Overview

DailyFitCompanion is a calorie and macro tracking app that helps you monitor your daily nutrition goals. This Privacy Policy describes how the app handles your information.

Simple summary: DailyFitCompanion does not collect, transmit, store, or share any of your personal data. Everything you enter — food logs, calorie goals, macro targets, notes, and weekly plans — stays entirely on your device.

2. No Data Collection

We do not collect any data from you. This includes:

  • No personal information (name, email, age, location)
  • No health or nutritional data
  • No usage analytics or behavioral tracking
  • No crash reports or diagnostics sent to us
  • No device identifiers
  • No advertising identifiers

We have no servers, no accounts, and no backend that receives any information from your app.

3. Local Storage Only

All data you enter in DailyFitCompanion — including your food logs, custom foods, calorie goals, macro targets (protein, carbs, fat), notes, and weekly plans — is stored exclusively on your device using iOS local storage.

This data is never uploaded, synced, or transmitted anywhere. It is only accessible to you on your device.

If you delete the app, all data is permanently removed from your device. We cannot recover it because we never had access to it in the first place.

4. Third-Party Services

DailyFitCompanion does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics services, advertising networks, or data brokers. No third party receives any information through the use of this app.

5. Children's Privacy

Because DailyFitCompanion does not collect any data from anyone, it is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 or under any applicable age of digital consent.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we ever make changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and notify users via an in-app notice. Our commitment to not collecting your data will remain unchanged.
